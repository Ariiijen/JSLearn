What is JavaScript?

JavaScript is a scripting language that enables you to create dynamically updating content, control multimedia, animate images, and pretty much everything else.

Created in just 10 days in May 1995 by Brendan Eich at Netscape, it became a standard in 1997 when adopted by ECMA â€” hence the official name ECMAScript.

Versions: ES1, ES2, ES3, ES5, ES6. Since 2016, versions are named by year (ES2016, ES2017...).
